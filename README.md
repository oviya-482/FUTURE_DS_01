# FUTURE_DS_01
Global Superstore Sales Performance analysis

📊 Sales & Revenue Performance Analysis
This repository contains data analysis and visualization of sales, revenue, and profitability trends across categories, products, and regions. The goal is to highlight business insights through structured charts and key metrics.

📂 Project Structure

monthly_revenue_trend.csv → Monthly revenue data (2011–2014).

yearly_revenue_trend.csv → Yearly revenue summary.

category_performance.csv → Revenue, profit, and orders by category.

regional_performance.csv → Regional revenue, profit, and margin analysis.

top_selling_products.csv → Top 10 products by revenue.

top_subcategories.csv → Sub-category performance breakdown.

📈 Visualizations
Monthly Revenue Trend → Line chart showing growth and seasonal peaks.

Yearly Revenue → Bar chart highlighting consistent year-over-year growth.

Category Revenue → Comparison of Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.

Regional Revenue Performance → Revenue distribution across global regions.

Regional Profit Margin % → Profitability comparison by region.

Top 10 Products by Revenue → Horizontal bar chart of best-selling products.

🔑 Key Insights
Peak Month: November 2014 with ~$402K in sales.

Peak Year: 2014 with ~$3.2M revenue.

Top Category: Technology (~$3.74M revenue).

Top Product: Apple Smart Phone (Full Size).

Top Region (Revenue): Central (~$2.07M).

Best Margin Region:  Canada (27.6% profit margin).
